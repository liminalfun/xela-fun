I'm a medium-agnostic artist and designer living in Montréal/Tiohtiá:ke interested in affective experiences of relation at the intersection of technology, society, and culture.
- - - - -
I like making music with buttons, knobs, and interfaces, singing with electronics, dancing, being literate, and crying. I like to balance improvisation with prewritten structure. I enjoy performing, but it depends. Sometimes I make music alone. Sometimes I make music with other people. Sometimes I make music for other people's films, dance performances, theatre performances, installations, etc. My artistic practice is corrolary to my research, design, and community work these days. Feeling demands the approach and execution, I guess.
